How to Use This Shop

Guidelines for ordering and understanding the work

Order

  • Each work is presented as a singular offering.
  • Select your piece and proceed to checkout.
  • Payment is processed securely via Stripe.
  • You will receive a confirmation once the order is complete.

Shipping

  • All works ship from Los Angeles.
  • Original works: dispatched within 3–7 business days.
  • Prints: produced and shipped within 5–10 business days.
  • United States: 3–7 days delivery.
  • International: 7–14 days delivery.
  • Tracking is provided with every shipment.

Packaging

  • Each work is handled with care and intention.
  • Originals are shipped flat or reinforced depending on their structure.
  • Prints are protected in archival-safe packaging—flat or rolled.

Shipping Costs

  • Shipping is calculated at checkout based on destination and scale of the work.

International Orders

  • Orders may be subject to customs duties or local taxes.
  • These charges are determined by your country and are the responsibility of the collector.

Color & Image

  • The digital image is a reference.
  • Variations in tone, depth, and texture may occur between screen and physical work.
  • These nuances are part of the translation from image to object.

Original Works

  • Original works are unique.
  • They carry the marks of process—material, gesture, and time.
  • Each piece is signed and exists as a singular object.

Prints

  • Prints are produced using archival materials and professional processes.
  • Each print is made to order.
  • Subtle variations may occur as part of the production method.

Copyright

  • Acquisition grants ownership of the physical work only.
  • All intellectual and reproduction rights remain with the artist.
  • No reproduction, modification, resale, or commercial use is permitted without written consent.
  • The artist retains the right to reinterpret or develop any work into future forms.

Returns & Damage

  • Original works are final.
  • Prints may be replaced only if they arrive damaged or defective.
  • If damage occurs, contact within 3 days with photographic documentation.
  • All shipments are insured.
